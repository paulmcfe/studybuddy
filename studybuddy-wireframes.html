üìù Interaction Notes

Card Flip: Tap/click anywhere on the card to flip between question and answer. No explicit "flip" button needed ‚Äî the entire card is the tap target.
Got it vs. Study More: "Got it" generates a new card on a potentially different topic within the selected scope. "Study More" generates another card on the same topic for deeper reinforcement.
Chat Context: When opened from a flashcard, the chat knows the current card's question, answer, and topic. The agent can reference this context: "I see you're looking at a card about X..."
Responsive Breakpoints:
‚Ä¢ Mobile: < 640px ‚Äî Single column, full-screen cards
‚Ä¢ Tablet: 640px ‚Äì 1024px ‚Äî Two columns (sidebar + card)
‚Ä¢ Desktop: > 1024px ‚Äî Three columns when chat is open
Empty State / Fallback: If RAG returns insufficient content, the agent generates cards from LLM knowledge and adds a subtle indicator: "Based on general AI knowledge" vs "From your study materials."
